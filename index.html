Who Are We
The 'Goshala' Corporation is a non-profit organization. The Corporation is organized exclusively for the purpose of creating a sanctuary for cows and bulls to live out their lives completely free from slaughter. Our Endevour
Work with local farmers to purchase or maintain retired cows to the end of their days. How you can Help
Volunteer in Activities. Spread the awareness. Help raise funds. Adopt a cow.

Our Mission

Our mission is to protect cows by educating society with the significance of cow based on Vedic scriptures; thus develop a culture within society where animals are protected and treated like a living being.

Go Raksha Punya Partnership program is a cow protection program that we practice at Goshala Farm, which saves cows from being slaughtered. It allows them to live out their golden years in peace at our farm.
