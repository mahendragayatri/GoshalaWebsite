Who Are We
The 'Goshala' Corporation is a non-profit organization. The Corporation is organized exclusively for the purpose of creating a sanctuary for cows and bulls to live out their lives completely free from slaughter. Our Endevour
Work with local farmers to purchase or maintain retired cows to the end of their days. How you can Help
Volunteer in Activities. Spread the awareness. Help raise funds. Adopt a cow.

Summer Watercolor Activity Workshop

Goshala Iowa present Summer Watercolor drawing event. all art enthusiasts are welcome to participate at this program where we will have fun learning to make watercolor decorated cards and posters. The program is meant for ages 9 to 99.

Program 1 (Kids age 6-8) - Dates: 6/26(Mon), 7/10 (Mon), 7/17(Mon), 7/22(Sat - 4 PM), 7/23(Sun - 4 PM), 7/24(Mon) Time: Weekdays @5.45 PM. Class will be for 1 hour duration

Program 2 (Ages 9 and above)- Dates: 6/27(Tue), 7/11 (Tue), 7/18(Tue), 7/22(Sat - 2 PM), 7/23(Sun - 2 PM), 7/25(Tue) Time: Weekdays @5.45 PM. Class will be for 1 hour duration

Future programs - Please register and we will notify you for future dates.

Course Level - Beginner to Intermediate

Event @ Cedar Rapids
Fees $20 for all classes or $5 each class

Each participant would bring their watercolor box and drawing pad. Other details will be emailed to the enrolled participants. Seats are limited therefore please enroll ASAP. The participants will receive event participation certificate on completion.
